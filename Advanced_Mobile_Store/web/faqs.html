Top 10 Frequently asked questions

    • We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, and net banking. We also offer cash on delivery.

  • How long does delivery take?

    • Delivery times vary based on your location and the product ordered. You can expect your order to arrive within 3-7 business days.

  • Do you offer international shipping?

    • We’re sorry, but currently, we do not offer international shipping. Our services are only available within the country. We appreciate your understanding and we’re constantly working to expand our services. Please keep checking back for updates. Thank you for your interest in our online mobile store.

  • What is your return policy?

    • We offer a 30-day return policy on all our products. The product must be in its original condition and packaging.

  • What if the product I want is out of stock?

    • If a product is out of stock, you can choose to be notified when it becomes available again.

    • There are no hidden charges. Any potential customs duties or taxes are not included in the price and are the responsibility of the customer.

  • How can I contact customer service?

    • You can reach our customer service team by email at support@onlinemobilestore.com or by phone at 1-800-123-4567.

  • How secure is my personal information?

    • We take your privacy very seriously. All personal information is stored securely and is not shared with third parties.

  • How can I leave a review?

    • You can leave a review on the product page of the item you purchased. We appreciate your feedback!

  • How can I track my order?

    • Once your order has been shipped, you will receive an email with tracking information.
